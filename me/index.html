About Me

I am a versatile Full-Stack Developer at New York University pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Integrated Digital Media and a Master of Science in Computer Science, with an expected graduation date of December 2023.

Before getting into college, I have been a self-taught designer with well-rounded experiences in graphic, web, and UI/UX design. Nonetheless, programming has also been a hobby of mine since high school, and I have written a few interesting scripts in my free time for fun.

After taking both some UX and digital design classes and some web development and computer science classes in college, however, I have started to realize that I don’t want to just stop at creating user persona or Figma mockups—I want to build fully functional apps and websites in code that users can actually use on real devices.

Since then, I have joined NYU IT as Student Web Developer and maintained and delivered numerous tools and modules that are being used by the university in production, from front-end web components to back-end mailers and QA automators, and I have self-learnt multiple languages and frameworks such as SCSS, Node.js, Vue.js, AWS, and PHP along the way.

Inside and outside the classroom, I have worked on several full-stack web development projects, mostly using React.js or Vue.js for front end, Node.js and Express.js for back end and RESTful APIs, and Firebase or MongoDB for database. I have also developed a couple of iOS and watchOS applications using Swift, SwiftUI, and ARKit.

Besides, I am a teaching assistant for Object Oriented Programming in C++ and I have worked as a tutor for Data Structures and Algorithms in Python.
